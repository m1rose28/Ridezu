Ridezu makes carpooling economical for both riders and drivers.

  • Riders Pay only $0.10 per mile, and a low $0.25 per trip fee. This represents an average commute savings of 25%!
  • Drivers Collect up to $0.10 per mile, per passenger. Over a year this could be hundreds or thousands of dollars!
  • Multi-Rider Discount Enjoy higher savings when there are two or more riders in the car 10% - Two riders 20% - Three of more riders
  • Lower Pricing on Tolls Each passenger in the car pays a pro-rata share of a toll fees (if any) - this reduces your toll fees by at least 50%!
  • Lower Pricing on Parking Each passenger in the car pays a pro-rata share of parking (if any) - this reduces your parking fees by at least 50%!
  • Late Fee No one likes being late. If you're late, you'll buy anyone you made late a cup of coffee (3.00)! This will be charged automatically to your account. If someone makes you late, you'll receive a credit ($3.00). This will be credited automatically to your account.
  • No Show Being a no show is really bad. If you're a no show your account will be debited $10. Multiple no shows will subject your account to termination. If your driver does not show, your account will be credited $10.
