Is there anything new in the Reward Points* program?

Yes! Now you can earn points to use at Vons gas stations and at participating Chevron and Texaco stations.

  • You can use up to 200 points to receive a 20¢ per gallon Reward for a single fill-up at a Chevron or Texaco station.
  • You can use up to 1000 points to receive a $1 per gallon Reward for a single fill-up at a Vons gas station.

How are Reward Points* earned?

It’s easy to earn Reward Points* just by shopping at Vons with your VonsClub Card:

  • Every $1 spent on groceries* = 1 point
  • Every $1 spent on qualifying gift cards* = 2 points
  • Every $1 spent on pharmacy items* = 1 point

How do Reward Points* save money on gas?

Every 100 points gets you a 10¢ per gallon Reward for a single fill-up at a Vons gas station or participating Chevron or Texaco station. You can use your points in increments of 100 and use up to 200 points for a single fill-up up at a participating Chevron or Texaco station, and up to 1000 points for a single fill-up at a Vons gas station of 25 gallons or less and a limit of 1 vehicle per transaction:

  • 100 points = 10¢ per gallon Reward
  • 200 points = 20¢ per gallon Reward
  • 1000 points = $1 per gallon Reward (Vons gas stations only)

Gas Rewards only apply to Chevron, Texaco and Vons branded products.

Can I use my Reward Points* immediately?

Your Reward Points* are available for use at participating Chevron or Texaco stations 24 hours after earning them and at Vons gas stations any time after earning them.

How do I keep track of my points?

You can track your points on your store receipts or by registering online at Vons.com and clicking the My Account link at the top of the page.

Do my Rewards expire?

Yes, Rewards expire at the end of the following month in which they were earned. For example, Rewards earned in October can be used through November 30th. Points earn Rewards in increments of 100 (100 points = 10¢ per gallon Reward). At the end of each month, any points less than 100 expire.

Which Gift Card purchases earn points?

Qualifying Gift Card purchases made with your VonsClub Card earn points. Excluded are American Express, PayPower, MasterCard, My Choice, Vons, Visa, Only 1 Visa, NetSpend and RE-loadit Gift Cards.

Which pharmacy purchases earn points?

As of August 1, 2012, every $1 spent on pharmacy items* with your VonsClub Card earns 1 point. This includes your out-of-pocket costs for new, transferred and refilled prescriptions.

Are there limits to how many points can be earned?

No, you can earn an unlimited number of points during each month and your points can be used through the end of the following month. Each month is a separate earning period; previous and current months’ points do not combine.

Back to Top
Are there limits to how many points can be used?

Yes, there is a limit of 200 points for a 20¢ per gallon Reward in a single fill-up (2 Rewards for 10¢ per gallon each) at a Chevron or Texaco station, and a limit of 1000 points for a single fill-up (10 Rewards for 10¢ per gallon each) at a Vons gas station. However, any additional Rewards you have earned can still be used through the end of the following month in which they were earned. Savings are limited to 25 gallons in a single fill-up or limits placed on your payment card by your financial institution, which may be lower. Limit of 1 vehicle per transaction.

How do I redeem my Reward Points* at a Chevron or Texaco station?

Just follow the pump screen prompts to accept and redeem your Rewards.

Yes, you can choose not to use your VonsClub Card to redeem Rewards at the gas station.
